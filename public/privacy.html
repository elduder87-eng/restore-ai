Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026 · Last Updated: March 14, 2026

Restore ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Restore application and website at mappedknowledge.ai. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

By using Restore, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address through our authentication provider (Clerk).
  • Conversation Data: The questions and messages you send to the Restore Guide are processed to generate AI responses and to build your knowledge galaxy.
  • Galaxy Data: Your knowledge nodes, mastery levels, and learning progress are stored to provide a persistent experience across sessions and devices.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and session duration.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the Restore experience
  • To generate AI responses through our language model provider (OpenAI)
  • To save and restore your knowledge galaxy across devices
  • To improve our service and develop new features
  • To communicate with you about your account or service updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Restore:

  • Clerk — Authentication and user management. clerk.com/privacy
  • OpenAI — AI language model for the Restore Guide. openai.com/privacy
  • Vercel — Hosting and infrastructure. vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Your conversation data is sent to OpenAI to generate responses. OpenAI's data practices are governed by their privacy policy. We do not sell your data to OpenAI or any third party for advertising purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your galaxy data is stored securely using Vercel KV (powered by Upstash Redis). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your account and galaxy data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Children's Privacy

Restore is designed for users of all ages, including students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
